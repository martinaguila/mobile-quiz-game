How to play
Mechanics
1. Player has only 3 trials to finish the game.
2. Player is given 15 seconds to answer the current question.
3. Game is over if player used all its trials or 15 seconds lapsed.
4. Unlock new levels every 20 questions.
5. Score is tallied on game over or when the game finished.
Gameplay
1. Answer questions to go to the next one.
2. Player looses 1 trial on every wrong answer.
