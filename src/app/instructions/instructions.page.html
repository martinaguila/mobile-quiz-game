How to play
Mechanics
1. Player has only 3 trials to finish 1 category.
2. Player is given 15 seconds to answer each question.
3. Player looses 1 trial on every wrong answer or 15 seconds expires.
Gameplay
1. Select 3 categories of dificulty.
2. Answer questions to go to the next one.
3. Need to have 10 correct answers to finish the game.
